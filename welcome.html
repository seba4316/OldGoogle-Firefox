Welcome to OldGoogle Search

Experience Google search with nostalgic designs from the past. Choose your preferred style below:

1998 - Classic Google
2009 - Clean & Simple
2010 - Sidebar Era
2011 - Modern Classic
2013 - Flat Design
Access the OldGoogle homepage anytime at: https://oldgoogle-home/

About OldGoogle

This extension uses neocities' Google reskin (oldgoogle.neocities.org) to provide a nostalgic Google search experience with designs from different eras. Select your preferred style above to customize your OldGoogle search experience.

You can always return to this page by clicking the OldGoogle icon in your browser toolbar.

Set OldGoogle as Default Search Engine

1 Open Firefox Settings by clicking the menu (≡) and selecting "Settings"
2 In the left sidebar, click "Search"
3 Under "Default Search Engine", select "OldGoogle" from the dropdown menu
4 Close Settings. OldGoogle is now your default search engine!

Set OldGoogle as Home Page

1 Open Firefox Settings by clicking the menu (≡) and selecting "Settings"
2 In the left sidebar, click "Home"
3 Select "Custom URLs" and enter: https://oldgoogle-home/
4 Close Settings. OldGoogle Home is now your home page!
